8:00 A.M.

Extended Registration, Display Booths, Breakfast and Girl Talk

At 8 a.m the event will kick off with extended registration and breakfast. Anyone who was not able to pay in advance will be able to pay at the door and also get registered for the event. Also during this time, the participants in this conference get to enjoy a nice leisurely breakfast complete with engaging conversations with other particpants. There are also booths from local vendors, so feel free to wonder around and get your fill!

9:00 A.M.

Welcome: Opening Prayer, Praise, Worship, and Dance

At this point in the conference, we will all convene into the main sanctuary in order to mark the start of the learning and fellowship. We will begin with the opening prayer delivered by #### . From that point we will transition into a time where worship, praise, and reverence will be flooding from the sanctuary. This wave of praise will be our marker to signal the start of the conference.

9:15 A.M.

Introductions: Skit/Slide Projection

At 9:15 we will continue with the conference with a short skit and from that point go into a slideshow. This slideshow will contain the workshops that are scheduled for the day. The workshops will be broken down to the information presented at each one as well who is in charge of teaching that certain section of the conference. So if you still have no idea what workshop you would like to attend be sure not to miss this informational event!

10:30 A.M.

Break / Display Booths Open

After the workshops there will be a chance to stretch our legs. There will be a break in order for everyone to regather themselves for the main event that follows soon after the break. During the break the display booths, are open again. So feel free to indulge again, but not too much because as we will whet our appetite with the Word soon after!

11:00 A.M.

Reconvene

After the break there is a 15 minute grace period in which all participants in the conference should make their way back to the main sanctuary for the main speaker. This event will be the calm before the storm of praise that will fill up and run out of the church's four walls!

11:15 A.M.

Keynote Speaker

This is the moment that all of our fellowship has been for. We will come together in order to get the main piece of the bread that is the Word. The speaker will be giving a sermon based on the premise of the conference, Line Up With The Word. The sermon will be given the main sanctuary. You do not want to miss this experience. This will be an explosive time in which we will feel the grce of god and come to a better understanding of our relationship with him. If you are who you say you are...Line Up With The Word!

12:15 P.M.

End of Keynote Speaker / Outside Fellowship
