Introducing the Woman of God

Dr. Cheri C. McCottrell-James

a woman who is after God's own heart; diligently seeking to win souls for Christ.

Dr. McCottrell-James is the daughter of Cornelius McCottrell and the late Patricia (Terrell) McCottrell. A native of a small community in southern Illinois known as Centreville, she was born in St. Louis, Illinois. Dr. McCottrell-James is a product of four universities.

  • Illinois State University at Normal, Illinois
  • Webster University at Webster Grove, Missouri
  • Anchor Theological Seminary and Bible Institue of Texarkana, Arkansas
  • Master's Divinity University (Gateway Biblical Counseling Center) at Indianapolis, Indiana

From her universities she has gained one Bachelor's degree, two Master degrees, and a Ph.D. In addition to her degrees, she is also a licensed and ordained minister of the gospel.

Working in many capacities of the church thoughout her life has allowed her to minister to all ages.

  • Minister Instructor
  • Youth Supervisor
  • Vacation Bible School Teacher
  • Minsterial Supervisor
  • Pastoral Support
  • Founder/Director of the Life Appreciation Drama Ministry
  • President/Director of Choirs
  • Prison Ministry
  • Chaplain at local hospitals
  • District Missionary for the Missouri Midwest Ecclesiastical Jurisdiction
Just to name a few.

Her work history with the state of Illinois has allowed her to assist abused and neglected children. She has also sponsored a child in the country of Uganda, humbly assisting in the physical and spiritual needs of that population. In addition to her many works, she is an Adjunct Professor at Lindenwood University.

Dr. McCottrell-James is the founder of Greater New Season International Ministries and in addition co-pastoring at Tabernacle of Deliverance Outreach Ministries, where she serves diligently beside her husband Pastor H. Randy James. Blessed with the gifts of evangilism, healing, deliverance, prophecy, and music, God has opened the door for her to minister to various denominations and organizations across the country.

Dr. Cheri C. McCottrell-James is an accomplished author of the book, "If you are who you say you are...Line Up With The Word". She is also in the process of the second edition of this book. The next project for Dr. McCottrell-James is her book, "The devil tried to kill me... But God Said Not So!" Available in the near future.

Available for preaching, Praise and Worship, Bible Teaching, Christian Counseling Behavioral Training, Workshops, among many other services.
